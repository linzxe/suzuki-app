Welcome!

Our program offers instruction in violin, viola and cello to students age 3 and older. Besides individual lessons, students participate in weekly group and theory classes. After mastering the fundamental skills, students participate in Beginning Orchestra. Students move on to the Intermediate and then Advanced Orchestra as their skills progress. Advanced students also participate in Chamber Music Ensembles and String Choirs.
